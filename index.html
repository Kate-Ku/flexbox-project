Hi. I'm Kate.

I am a Responsive Designer & Developer who thrives on visual storytelling. When I'm not working, pursuing side projects or getting lost in the rabbit hole of blogs & books, I can be found travelling, surfing and learning new languages.

I was born and raised in Switzerland, but I have roots in the Philippines. When I got to know my relatives and the culture, I simply fell in love with the country.

My passion is surfing. I started when I was 19 years old and without having friends practising this sport I always felt that passion deep inside until I moved to my beautiful island home surrounded by amazing waves.

Language connects! I realized very quickly that language can build bridges between cultures. Therefore I'm trying to learn all the languages necessary to live and communicate with the people I live with.
